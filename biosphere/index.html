Our answer is Biosphere Network!

About Us

We’re Сreating a World where all Human Needs will be Satisfied using open environmental information We are a team consisting of experienced high-level specialists in the fields of ecology, engineering, software development and business management.

Our mission

To provide people with an objective and independent information about state of environment to preserve their health and health of their loved ones.

Our aim

To create global economically independent decentralized IoT system, namely to collect, store and supply data of objective environmental monitoring.

Our task

To create device (biostations) with sensors, decentralized open blockchain platform and services (based on data received from platform).

How It Works

Product

Biosphere Station is designed in format of device for self-installation

  • on territory of households
  • on exterior walls of apartment buildings
  • in fields and open sun forest areas
  • on drifting or unmanned water platforms

Biosphere station owner will be able to design it both independently and buy a ready-made device.
