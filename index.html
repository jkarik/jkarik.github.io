What is Tael?

Tael is a loyalty token used in the techrock ecosystem of verifiable safe and authentic products.

The Flow of Tael?

Consumers are rewarded with Tael through the simple acts of purchasing and scanning

Techrock uses Tael to incentivize consumers to scan the label, assuring the collective security

Merchants purchase (and accept) Tael to utilize as a marketing tool and gain valuable insights

Exchanges give consumers full control over their rewards, allowing them to gain real value

Tael is added to your account by purchasing a product, referring your friends, verifying authenticity by scanning the techrock label, or participating in activities.

It's that easy.

Real usage in Techrock channel

Each product is protected by unique NFC and blockchain-backend anti-counterfeiting technology.

We focus on protecting products that matter the most

Next generation loyalty system for brands & merchants

Reward in a new way

Unlike other loyalty tokens, Tael rewards translate into real value. No expiration dates, no exchange restrictions, no fuss. Just real value for real actions.

Loyalty program as a service

Merchants are given a built-in loyalty program and a new direct marketing channel to their customers, without the usual costs of maintenance.

Maximize the power of data

Brands gain deep consumer insights impossible to acquire in other ecosystems, such as proof of consumption.
